About

Welcome to my professional portfolio! I am a professor of Strategic Management and Entrepreneurship at SUNY Oswego and I currently serve as Chair of the Marketing & Management Department for the School of Business. Prior to working at SUNY Oswego, I taught at the University of Maryland and served as a visiting professor for numerous universities in Europe and South Asia.

In addition to being a seasoned academic, I've also pubslihed numerous books and several journal articles related to entrepreneurship and business management. I served as the Chair for INFORMS Technology Management (2002-04) and an officer of the Academy of Management TIM Division (2000-05). I've also co-founded the North American Innovation Research Network (NAIRNet) and I'm currently the Head (PI) of Global Entrepreneurship Monitor, Pakistan.
